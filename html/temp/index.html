Welcome


SlugApp is a centralized web application made for UCSC students in which they can post and view events, dining hall menus, and loop bus locations.
    EVENTS Post and view a list of events that are happening around campus!
    DINING View dining hall menus!
    SLUGMAP Track campus loop buses!
    ABOUT SlugApp was made by team members Simba Khadder, Karol Josef Bustamante, Kyle Young, Isaiah Solomon, Andrew Lee, and Megan Nguyen.
